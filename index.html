About Me

My name is Florence René, and I am a PMP-certified project and product leader with a career that spans operations, Finance, and IT.

I am driven by a genuine desire to influence people and organizations to achieve desired outcomes by creating clarity, scalable systems, and structures built on excellence and accountability. Outside of work, I pour that same energy into leadership development, community organizations, and the people around me.

My goal is to move into a leadership role where I can lead a team tasked with bridging the gap between business and IT to solve complex problems.

I strive to be authentic and respectful in every interaction. I refuse to let others define my worth, and I am grateful for the challenges and the journey of self-discovery that have brought me to where I am today.

Leadership Philosophy: R.E.S.P.E.C.T.

Respect is fundamental to how I lead. It is not just about politeness; it is a genuine commitment to understanding and valuing the people I work with. Do unto others as you would have them do unto you is one of my favorite sayings, and these are the tenets I lead by:

  • Empowering Collaboration: creating a safe space for open communication and diverse perspectives.
  • Clear Expectations and Accountability: setting clear goals and holding myself and others responsible.
  • Trust and Empowerment: leading with transparency, integrity, and support for autonomy.
  • Effective Communication: prioritizing open dialogue, active listening, and clarity.
  • Adaptability and Resilience: embracing change with a solution-oriented mindset.
  • Continuous Learning and Development: staying curious and learning from successes and failures alike.
  • Recognition and Appreciation: celebrating milestones and valuing the efforts of others.
